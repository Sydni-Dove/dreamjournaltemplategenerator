How to Use This Generator
  1. 1
    Upload or paste your notes Drag in a PDF, Word doc, or TXT file, or paste your prophetic notes into the text box above. You can also skip this step and fill in the fields manually.
  2. 2
    Click Generate The generator reads your notes and automatically fills in the date, word, scripture, source, and other fields. Your journal preview will appear below the form.
  3. 3
    Review and edit freely Use the "← Back to Editor" button to adjust or add to any field, then click Generate again to refresh your preview.
  4. 4
    Choose your Notes Pages Select Lined or Blank from the Notes Page Style dropdown, then choose how many pages to add after each entry (1–10). Set to None if you don't need extra writing space.
  5. 5
    Print or Save as PDF Click Print to send to your printer. To save as a PDF, choose "Save as PDF" as the destination in your print dialog.
  6. 6
    Save as PDF Downloads a formatted PDF directly to your computer — no print dialog needed. You can also use the Print button and choose "Save as PDF" as the destination for the highest-quality output.
Tip: If you paste multiple dated entries (e.g. 2.23.26 … 2.24.26 …), the generator automatically creates a separate journal page for each date.
